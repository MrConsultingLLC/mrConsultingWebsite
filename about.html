About

An engineer's depth, with a leader's perspective.

MR Consulting LLC is a solo electrical engineering practice — focused on medical devices, and founded on the belief that the best technical work comes from people who have actually done it, from schematic to shipped product.

Background

Nearly two decades of building real products.

I'm an electrical engineer with nearly twenty years of product development experience. Most recently, I spent roughly a decade specializing in infrared-based temperature measurement at a medical device company in the Boston area — work that spanned analog and digital circuit design, embedded firmware, sensor integration, and the long road from a working prototype to a manufacturable, compliant product.

My career has also included experience in defense and aviation. It shaped how I think about reliability and rigor, though medical device engineering is where my focus lives today.

What sets the work apart isn't a single specialty — it's range. I can sit at the bench and debug a board, write the firmware that drives it, and then walk onto the manufacturing floor and understand why a design decision made six months ago is now causing yield problems.

From individual contributor to the leadership table

I've operated on both sides of the org chart. As a Director of Engineering, I led engineering teams of up to roughly ten people. As an Operations Manager, I ran teams of up to about twenty-five — owning hiring, performance management, and cross-functional project leadership.

That means I understand both the engineer's perspective and leadership's priorities. I've sat at the table with leadership teams, and I can engage your executives as a peer — not just as a contractor taking a ticket.

The differentiator: design-to-manufacturing

The gap between a design that works in the lab and a design that can be made, reliably and at volume, is where products quietly fail. I understand how upstream engineering decisions ripple downstream to the manufacturing floor — and how to make those decisions well the first time. It's a rare combination, and it's the heart of what I offer.
